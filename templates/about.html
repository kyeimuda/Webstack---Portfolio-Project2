Workerfy

Workerfy is a project developed for a portfolio project by Bernard Kyei Mensah at the ALX/HOLBERTON SE programme.

Workerfy is a web application that seeks to breech the gap between people finding worker (civil workers) and the workers who hope to reach more customers.

Workerfy helps workers (civil workers) to create a digital account on its platform, shares the account profile for others to be able to reach tge worker via the internet.

Workerfy helps individuals who need civil workers but cannot find one due to various reasons such as work and time constraints and also location disadvantage e.t.c.

At your comfort one can browse through the workers profile, if you like their works via images, you can contact then using information provided on their profiles.


Workerfy, simply workers at your doorstep.
